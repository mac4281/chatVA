RESPONSE:

The documentation required for a prior approval for an IRRRL is a lenders cover or transmittal letter, a VA Form 26-8937, a statement signed by the Veteran acknowledging the effect of the refinancing loan, a VA Form 26-8923, a CAIVRS, a URLA with revised VA Form 26-1802a, a VA Form 26-0503, a VA Form 26-0592, documentation to verify that the cause of the delinquency has been corrected, a credit report, a current pay stub and verification of current employment, a VA Form 26-6393, documentation of the cost of energy efficiency improvements, and any other necessary documents.
