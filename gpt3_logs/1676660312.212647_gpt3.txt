Once approved by VA, the Regional Loan Center (RLC) will issue a VA Certificate of Commitment. Ensure compliance with any conditions listed on the Certificate of Commitment before closing the loan. To obtain the loan guaranty certificate, the closing package must be uploaded into WebLGY, for the RLCs final review.
