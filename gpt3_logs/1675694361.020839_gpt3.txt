RESPONSE:

To submit your alarm application by fax, you will need to first select the Forms option from the left navigation panel. Next, select the Custom Input Forms option. This will expand the selection to include all of the custom input forms that are available. Select the form you wish to fax from this list. Once the form is open, you will need to enter the information into the fields provided. When you are finished, select the Fax option from the top of the screen. This will open a new window where you can enter the fax number and any other required information. Once you have entered all of the required information, select the Send Fax button to submit your application.
