If a lender is unable to obtain an electronic LGC, the following documents should be uploaded into WebLGY, in the order listed: 1. Lenders cover or transmittal letter. 2. VA Form 26-0286, VA Loan Summary Sheet. 3. COE. 4. VA Form 26-1820, Report and Certification of Loan Disbursement. 5. Closing Disclosure. 6.
