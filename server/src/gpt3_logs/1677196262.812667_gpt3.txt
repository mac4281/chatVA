RESPONSE:

According to Chapter 10, page 40 of the VA Pamphlet 26-7, Revised, if a lender determines that a title limitation or condition discovered prior to loan closing may affect eligibility, the lender must contact the appraiser to determine if the condition can be corrected. If the appraiser determines that the condition cannot be corrected, the lender must submit a request for a waiver to the RLC.
